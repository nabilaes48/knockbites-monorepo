## Introduction

Welcome to Cameron's Connect, the official mobile and web ordering platform for Cameron's 24-7 stores. This Privacy Policy explains how Highland Mills Snack Shop Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

By using Cameron's Connect, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide to Us

When you use Cameron's Connect, we collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

**For Order Placement:**
- **Name** - To identify your order when ready for pickup
- **Phone Number** - To contact you about your order status
- **Email Address** (optional) - For order confirmations and receipts

**For Account Creation (Optional):**
- **Email Address** - For account login and communication
- **Password** - Securely hashed and encrypted
- **Delivery Address** (if applicable) - For future delivery services

**Order Information:**
- Items ordered
- Special instructions or customizations
- Order timestamps
- Store location selected
- Payment information (if paying online - currently we accept payment at pickup only)

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Service, we automatically collect certain information:

**Device Information:**
- Device type (iPhone, iPad, Android, Web browser)
- Operating system version
- App version
- Unique device identifiers

**Usage Information:**
- Pages viewed
- Features used
- Time spent in app
- Search queries
- Menu items browsed

**Location Information:**
- Approximate location (city/zip code level) to show nearest Cameron's locations
- We DO NOT track your precise GPS location without explicit permission

### 3. Information from Third Parties

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

- **Supabase** - Our secure database and authentication provider
- **Vercel** - Web hosting infrastructure
- **Apple Push Notification Service** - For order status notifications (iOS only)

## How We Share Your Information

We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

### With Our Staff
- Store employees see your name and phone number to prepare and fulfill your order
- This information is only visible to staff at the specific store you're ordering from

### With Service Providers
We work with trusted third-party companies to provide our Service:

- **Supabase (Database & Auth)** - SOC 2 Type II compliant, stores data securely
- **Vercel (Web Hosting)** - Hosts our website infrastructure
- **Apple (Push Notifications)** - Delivers order status notifications on iOS

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and only use it for providing services to us.

### For Legal Reasons
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Court orders or legal processes
- Requests from government authorities
- Protection of our legal rights
- Prevention of fraud or illegal activity

### Business Transfers
If Cameron's Connect or Highland Mills Snack Shop Inc. is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred to the new owner.

## Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures:

### Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption in Transit** - All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Encryption at Rest** - Database contents are encrypted using AES-256
- **Secure Authentication** - Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt
- **Row Level Security** - Database access is restricted based on user roles

### Organizational Safeguards
- Staff access to customer data is limited to order fulfillment only
- Regular security audits of our infrastructure
- Employee training on data privacy best practices

### Limitations
While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
